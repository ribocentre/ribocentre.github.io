## Description
***

<font size=4>Ribosomes are the most critical macromolecules that widely exist in all three kingdoms of life, which catalyze the peptidyl transfer reaction to translate mRNA to proteins. It is the most abundant natural ribozyme with polymerase activity in nature.</font><br>
